What is Ski Touring?

Ski touring is one of my favorite ways to explore the mountains in the winter! It’s basically backcountry skiing—where you hike uphill using special gear (like skins and touring bindings) and then ski down in fresh, untouched snow. It’s a fun mix of challenge and adventure, and there’s nothing better than earning your turns. I’ve been touring for a few seasons now and love taking friends out and helping them get into it too!
